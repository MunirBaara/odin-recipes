Hummus Recipe

What is Hummus?

Hummus is basically a delicious breakfast dip. It tastes great with pita bread, kebabs, chicken, salads, and even chips. I love this dip is falafel wraps! This dip is so delicious. Hummus is made from chickpea, tahini and natural spices/ flavors such as lemon.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can Chickpea
  • 1/2 cup Tahini
  • 1/4 cup Fresh lemon juice
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 clove garlic
  • olive oil
  • 2-6 tbsp cold water

Steps to make your Palestenian Hummus:

  1. Wash your chickpeas. Juice your lemon, prep your garlic, spices, olive oil and tahini.

  2. In a blender (or food processor) combine all of your ingredients (except for the water) and blend until chunky.

    Begin by adding 2 tablspoons of the water and blend for a minutes, make sure that the hummus is smooth and fluffy but still thick. If you want add a bit more water until the desired consisitency.

  3. Add to a plate and smooth out with a wet spoon. Top with parsely, parika and a generous drizzle of olive oil.

    Enjoy!
